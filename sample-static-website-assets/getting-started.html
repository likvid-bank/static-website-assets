Getting Started

Installation

Online Banking App is available for download on iOS and Android. Simply visit the App Store or Google Play Store and search for "Online Banking App".

Registration

  1. Open the Online Banking App app.
  2. Click "Create Account".
  3. Enter your personal details and verify your identity with an OTP.
  4. Set up your security credentials, including a strong password and biometric authentication if supported.
