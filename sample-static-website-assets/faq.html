Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click on 'Forgot Password' on the login screen, and follow the instructions to reset your password via email or SMS.

Can I set spending limits on my accounts?

Yes, go to 'Settings' -> 'Spending Limits' and set your desired limits for transactions and withdrawals.
