Using the App

Account Management

To view your account balances, navigate to the home screen after logging in. Click on each account to view detailed transaction history.

Fund Transfers

To transfer funds, choose 'Transfer' from the main menu. Select between transferring to your own accounts or to other banks, enter the details, and confirm the transaction.

Bill Payments

Pay bills easily by adding payees under 'Payments'. Once a payee is added, select it and enter the payment amount to complete the process.
